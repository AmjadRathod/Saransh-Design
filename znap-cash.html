Project Description

Znap reached out to us to help them carry out all stages of product design with the goal of increasing consumer engagement and identify reasons for high drop off rates and no consumer & brand loyalty. We did a series of steps including UX Audit, Discovery and alignment of future business goals followed up by the UX and eventual visual design.
